About Matching Brackets

Welcome! My name is Joe, and Matching Brackets is where I help everyday computer users become more comfortable with Linux.

My goal isn't to impress you with complicated commands. It's to explain Linux in plain English and show you practical ways to use it every day.

Whether you're completely new to Linux or returning after years away, I hope you'll find something useful here.
